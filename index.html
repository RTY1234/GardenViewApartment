🔐 Smartlock Instructions

The apartment is equipped with a smartlock so you can self-check-in and check-out. There is a keypad near the entrance where you will need to provide a PIN code to unlock the door.

⚠️ Your PIN code will be sent separately on the day of arrival

How to use the smartlock:

  • To unlock: Enter your PIN code on the keypad
  • To lock/unlock from inside: Rotate the knob on the smartlock
  • To lock when going out: Press the arrow button on the keypad and it will immediately lock
